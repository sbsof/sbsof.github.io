Engineered for Modern Lift Professionals

Cranevis packs seven robust modules into one seamless interface, replacing heavy books and complex spreadsheets right in the field.

1. Data Center

Central storage for your lifting configurations. Safely record, edit, and instantly recall specific crane capacities, building measurements, and load matrices.

2. Sling Table

A dynamic rating encyclopedia covering wire rope slings, synthetic webbing, and chain configurations. Supports multi-grade selections with a seamless imperial-metric toggle.

3. Angle Camera

Use your phone’s camera system to lock in sling orientation angles. Snap a quick layout photo and overlay measuring protractors to assure dynamic hoisting tolerances.

4. Sling Tension

Enter load specs and apex geometries to compute individual line stress. Instantly verify if the sling tension stays safely inside operational limits.

5. Simulation Workspace

Configure telescopic or lattice boom models. Adjust surrounding structural boundaries and dimensions dynamically, scaling loads on-the-fly to test clearances.

6. Ground Bearing Pressure

Analyze ground outrigger forces in real time. Input Ground Bearing Capacity (GBC) guidelines to compute the necessary support pad areas, ensuring total structural safety.

7. Professional Lifting Plan Builder

Compile all your simulation telemetry, sling designs, and soil pressures into an instantly generated, print-ready, professional PDF Lifting Plan. Ready to share with your safety officers or print on-site.
